About Me

Hello! My name is Tandin Wangmo, and I come from Kanglung, Trashigang. I have four siblings, and being the middle one has taught me the importance of balance, understanding, and adaptability. I am a Buddhist, and my beliefs deeply influence the way I perceive life, inspiring me to live with compassion, mindfulness, and gratitude.

I have always had a passion for reading books, exploring new ideas, and learning about different cultures, as they broaden my perspective and help me connect with the world in meaningful ways. Writing is another pursuit I truly enjoy, as it allows me to express my thoughts creatively and reflectively. Alongside this, I like to play online games, which sharpen my strategic thinking, and I often engage in problem-solving activities that challenge my mind and keep me motivated in daily life.

My friends and family often describe me as curious, hardworking, and eager to learn, qualities I proudly embrace. In my free time, I enjoy listening to music, reflecting on life, and challenging myself with new experiences that encourage personal growth.
